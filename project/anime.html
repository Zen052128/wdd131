The Anime Adaptation

Lord of the Mysteries finally received its highly anticipated donghua adaptation, bringing the mysterious world of Beyonders, secret organizations, and ancient gods to life.

Official Trailer

Release

The adaptation was announced after years of anticipation from fans of the original web novel.

Genre

Mystery, Supernatural, Fantasy, Steampunk, Horror, Adventure, and Psychological.

Setting

A Victorian-inspired world filled with churches, Beyonder pathways, hidden organizations, and terrifying cosmic entities.

Why Read the Novel?

While the anime introduces the world beautifully, the novel contains deeper world-building, more detailed character development, and many mysteries that cannot fit within a single season.

  • Over 1400 chapters of story content.
  • Detailed Beyonder pathways.
  • Expanded Tarot Club interactions.
  • Rich world-building and lore.
