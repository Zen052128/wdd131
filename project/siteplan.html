Lord of the Mysteries

Website Planning Document

Site Name

Lord of the Mysteries

The website name is based on the popular Chinese fantasy mystery series Lord of the Mysteries. I chose this name because it is immediately recognizable to both existing fans and new viewers. The website will serve as a beginner-friendly guide that introduces visitors to the anime, donghua, characters, pathways, and lore while encouraging them to explore the original novel.

Optional Domain Name: lordofthemysterieshub.com

Site Purpose

This website provides information about the world of Lord of the Mysteries. Visitors can learn about major characters, Beyonder pathways, organizations, and important lore. The site is designed primarily for new viewers who are interested in understanding the anime and donghua adaptation and may wish to begin reading the original novel.

The website will include:

  • Character profiles
  • Beyonder pathway explanations
  • Anime and donghua information
  • World-building and lore guides
  • Novel recommendations for new readers

Scenarios

  • Who is Klein Moretti, and why is he important to the story?
  • What are Beyonder pathways, and how do they work?
  • What is the difference between the anime adaptation and the original novel?
  • Which Beyonder pathway should I learn about first as a new fan?

Color Schema

Deep Midnight Blue (#0B132B)

Used for the main background, hero sections, and navigation.

Antique Gold (#D4AF37)

Used for headings, navigation links, and highlights.

Dark Bronze (#6B4F2A)

Used for borders, cards, and decorative elements.

Fog Gray (#A9A9A9)

Used for secondary text and supporting content.

Typography

Cinzel Font Example

Used for headings, navigation menus, and major titles.

Cormorant Garamond Example

Used for quotes, lore sections, and special content.

Merriweather is used for body text and long-form reading content because it is highly readable and visually appealing.
